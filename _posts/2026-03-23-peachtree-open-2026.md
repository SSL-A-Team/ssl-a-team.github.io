RoboCup Small Size League returned to the United States for the first time in twenty years with this past weekend's Peachtree Open! With four teams and twenty four participants from across the country, the event was a successful launch of the new tournament. Our teams had a blast testing their systems and sharing knowledge with spectators.

Thank you to our participating teams: RoboJackets, The Bots, and TritonBots. And a special thanks to league Organizing Committee member Tobias for flying out to support our new event!

We look forward to inviting teams back in 2027 for the next Peachtree Open tournament!
